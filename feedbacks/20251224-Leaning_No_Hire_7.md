# Red Flag Evaluation (20251224)

**Rating**: Leaning_No_Hire

## Question

Describe a time when you delivered a project under a tight deadline.

## Answer

Situation: In my previous role as a software developer at a fintech company, we were tasked with developing a new feature for our mobile banking app that was critical for meeting an upcoming regulatory compliance deadline. The timeframe was tight, and there was no room for extension due to the regulatory nature of the project. Task: I took full ownership of the backend API development, which involved writing approximately 2,000 lines of code, designing the database schema, and creating all necessary database migrations. My responsibility was to ensure that the feature met all regulatory requirements and was delivered on time. Action: To tackle this challenge, I initiated a comprehensive planning session with my team to outline the project scope and break it down into manageable tasks. I prioritized these tasks based on their importance and dependencies. Recognizing the urgency, I implemented agile development practices, including daily stand-up meetings to track progress and identify blockers early. I coordinated closely with the compliance team to ensure we met all necessary regulations, handling all compliance-related technical requirements myself. When we encountered a setback due to a third-party API changing its authentication method, I took the lead in resolving the issue by contacting their support team, obtaining the new documentation, and rewriting the integration code within two days. I also made a strategic decision to simplify our authentication flow, which saved us about five days of development time, allowing us to focus on core functionalities. To keep the team aligned, I created a 'must-have' vs 'nice-to-have' list and set up daily check-ins to ensure we stayed on track. Result: As a result of my diligent work and effective coordination, we completed the development ahead of schedule, allowing for thorough testing and quality assurance. The feature was successfully launched on time, meeting all regulatory requirements, with a feature adoption rate of 65% among eligible users within the first month, an error rate of less than 0.1%, and a transaction success rate of 98.5%. The compliance team confirmed that we passed all audit checks, and user feedback was positive, averaging 4.2 out of 5 stars. I presented these metrics to management in a post-launch review, which was well-received. This experience reinforced the importance of strategic planning, agile methodologies, and clear communication in successfully managing and delivering projects under tight deadlines, while also highlighting my ability to take ownership and make impactful decisions.
