## Answer

During our discussion, I asked targeted questions that facilitated an open dialogue, allowing me to pinpoint the root causes of the inefficiencies. I made sure to document my optimization work in our project management tool with clear before/after metrics and included this work in my performance review, which my manager supported. Additionally, I wrote a technical blog post about the optimization techniques I used, which was published on our company's engineering blog, further solidifying my contributions and recognition within the team.
