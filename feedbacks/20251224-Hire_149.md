## Question

Tell me about a complex technical project you've worked on.

## Answer

In my previous role as a backend development lead at a data analytics company, I took complete ownership of developing a large-scale data processing and analysis platform capable of handling terabytes of data from diverse sources while delivering real-time analytics. I meticulously defined the project scope and architected the solution to align with our business objectives. After evaluating various technology stacks, I selected Apache Spark for its efficiency in processing large datasets, ultimately achieving a throughput of 15TB/hour against our target of 10TB/hour. This decision was influenced by thorough analysis, including metrics on latency, error rates, and resource utilization, which I tracked using real-time dashboards.

I personally designed the microservices architecture and wrote approximately 3,500 lines of Scala code for the core data processing pipeline. I also developed the data ingestion system that integrated multiple data sources, creating service communication patterns and API contracts between services. To ensure the platform's scalability and maintainability, I implemented clear service boundaries based on data domains, established independent CI/CD pipelines for each service, and ensured comprehensive documentation and monitoring were in place.

Collaboration was essential to our success. I proactively engaged with the front-end development team and data scientists to ensure our backend solutions met user interface requirements and analytical needs. One significant challenge we faced was optimizing the data ingestion process, which initially caused processing bottlenecks, taking eight hours to process data that should take one hour. I identified that the bottleneck was in our data joining strategy and implemented a more efficient join algorithm along with in-memory caching for frequently accessed data, which ultimately reduced processing time by 85%.

Throughout the project, I conducted extensive load testing early and often, testing each component as I built it. I also led architecture review sessions and ensured rigorous code reviews on all critical components, engaging in pair programming on the most complex parts. This hands-on approach allowed me to provide guidance to the team while fostering a collaborative environment.

The project culminated in a successful launch, where the platform exceeded our initial benchmarks by processing data 50% faster than anticipated. This enhancement enabled our clients to derive insights in real-time, significantly improving their decision-making capabilities. As a direct result of our collective efforts, we observed a 30% increase in client satisfaction scores, contributing to a 15% growth in our company‚Äôs revenue in the subsequent quarter.

Throughout this experience, I learned the importance of thoughtful architecture, proactive problem-solving, and the power of cross-functional teamwork. I navigated differing opinions on technical decisions by advocating for data-driven choices while remaining open to feedback, which fostered a collaborative environment. This project reinforced my commitment to ownership and leadership, as I successfully navigated complex technical challenges and drove the project to success while ensuring alignment with both business objectives and user needs. Additionally, I recognized the importance of continuous improvement and adaptability, understanding that my ability to lead cross-functional teams and deliver measurable results will be a significant asset in future endeavors.
