## Question

Tell me about a complex technical project you've worked on.

## Answer

In my previous role as a backend development lead at a data analytics company, I took full ownership of a complex project to develop a large-scale data processing and analysis platform capable of handling terabytes of data from diverse sources while delivering real-time analytics. I personally designed the overall microservices architecture and wrote the core data processing pipeline, which comprised about 3,500 lines of Scala code. I also created the data ingestion system that effectively managed multiple data sources, establishing service communication patterns and API contracts between services.

To ensure we met our ambitious performance goals, I conducted an exhaustive evaluation of various technology stacks and advocated for Apache Spark for distributed processing. I demonstrated that Spark would be faster to implement, more maintainable, and better suited for our needs compared to a custom solution proposed by a team member. I presented a detailed comparison with data and prototypes, which led to the team agreeing on Spark, ultimately saving us three months in development time.

Throughout the project, I implemented extensive load testing early and often, testing each component as I built it. I created comprehensive documentation, held architecture review sessions, and ensured rigorous code reviews on all critical components. My proactive approach to problem-solving was evident when I identified a bottleneck in our initial data processing approach, which was taking eight hours to process data that should have taken one hour. I implemented a more efficient join algorithm and used in-memory caching for frequently accessed data, reducing processing time by 85%. Additionally, I addressed issues with unreliable data sources by implementing a robust retry mechanism with exponential backoff and a fallback data source.

Collaboration was key to our success; I worked closely with the front-end development team and data scientists to ensure our backend solutions aligned seamlessly with user interface requirements and analytical needs. I tracked key metrics such as data processing throughput, latency, error rate, and resource utilization, achieving a throughput of 15TB/hour against a target of 10TB/hour, with a p95 latency under 2 seconds and an error rate kept under 0.1%. These metrics informed our decisions, such as optimizing the data serialization format when latency increased, and adding robust error handling when error rates rose under load.

The project culminated in a successful launch, where the platform exceeded our initial benchmarks by processing data 50% faster than anticipated. This improvement enabled our clients to derive insights in real-time, significantly enhancing their decision-making capabilities. As a direct result of our collective efforts, we observed a 30% increase in client satisfaction scores, which contributed to a 15% growth in our companyâ€™s revenue over the subsequent quarter.

Throughout this experience, I learned the importance of thoughtful architecture, proactive problem-solving, and the power of cross-functional teamwork. I also recognized the value of clear communication in navigating differing opinions on technical decisions. This project reinforced my commitment to ownership and leadership, as I navigated complex technical challenges and drove the project to success while ensuring alignment with both business and user needs. I am eager to apply these lessons and my experience in future projects to drive even greater impact.
